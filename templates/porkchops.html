Kickin' Pork and Potato Casserole

By Kick It Up

Ingredients

6 (1-inch-thick) boneless porkchops

1 Tbsp olive oil

1/2 (28 oz) hash browns, thawed

1 (10.5-oz) can cream of mushroom soup

1 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese

1/2 cup sour cream

1 scoop of Kick It Up Blend (2 scoops if you're feeling brave)

Cooking Instructions

1) Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Sprinkle pork lightly with salt and pepper. Cook pork chops in hot oil in large skillet over medium-high heat until browned. Remove from skillet.

2) Meanwhile, stir together potatoes, soup, cheese, sour cream, and Kick It Up Blend in large bowl; season with salt and pepper. Spoon mixture into a lightly greased baking dish.

3) Nestle pork chops in potato mixture; cover and bake 30 minutes or until pork chops are tender and potatoes are hot and bubbly.



Pairs great with Roasted Green Beans

Prehead oven to 400 degrees. Toss together trimmed green beans and oil on a rimmed baking sheet. Season lightly with salt and pepper.

Bake 15 minutes or until browned and tender.
