Our Famous Dip

By Kick It Up

Ingredients

1 (16-oz-can) refried beans

1 (8-oz) sour cream

1 block of cream cheese, softened

1 packet of taco seasoning

1 (8 oz) guacamole (mild)

Kick it Up Salsa

1 cup Mexican blend cheese

Bundle of green onions, chopped

1 (6-oz-can) black olives, chopped

Cooking Instructions

1) Mix Kick It Up Blend with Tomatoes of your choice. Follow instructions on back of bag.

2) After the mix is hydrated in the tomatoes layer ingredients in a 9 inch pan in the following order: refried beans, creams cheese mixed with taco seasoning, guacamole, sour cream, Kick It Up Salsa, and cheese.

3) Top with black olives and green onions

4) Refrigerate for 30 minutes or until desired coldness

5) Enjoy with tortilla chips or fritos!



Substitutions for black olives and green onions

Shredded Lettuce

Chopped Tomatoes

Chopped yellow onion
