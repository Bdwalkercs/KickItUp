Campfire Chili

By Kick It Up

Ingredients

1.5 lbs cooked ground chuck

2 can kidney beans

2 cans black beans

1 can rotel

3 scoops Kick It Up Salsa Blend

Tomato juice

Cooking Instructions

1) Cook ground chuck in pan for 10 minutes or until brown

2) Mix together beans, rotel, and ground chuck in crock pot

3) Pour tomato juice in mix until desired consitency is achieved

4) Cook on low for 8 hours adding salsa blend during the last 15 minutes of cook time



Pairs great with Kick It Up Corn Bread

Prepare 1 box of Jiffy Mix according to box directions

Add 3/4 scoop of blend and 1/2 cup of grated cheese of your choice

Bake as directed
